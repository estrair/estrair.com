About EstrAir

EstrAir is focused on understanding airway dysfunction in sleep-related breathing disorders. Our work explores physiological and structural contributors to airway collapse during sleep and potential pathways for innovative therapeutic approaches.

Therapeutic Innovation

Exploring strategies that address root causes of airway dysfunction.
