About EstrAir

EstrAir is exploring innovative approaches to addressing airway dysfunction in sleep-related breathing disorders. Our work focuses on understanding the structural and physiological mechanisms that contribute to airway collapse during sleep and identifying new therapeutic pathways to improve outcomes for patients.

The Problem

Obstructive Sleep Apnea

Obstructive sleep apnea affects millions worldwide and is associated with cardiovascular disease, cognitive impairment, and reduced quality of life.

Limited Therapies

Current treatments often rely on mechanical interventions rather than addressing the underlying airway physiology contributing to disease.

Unmet Need

There remains a significant need for innovative approaches that target the root causes of airway collapse during sleep.

Our Approach

Airway Physiology

Investigating the mechanisms underlying airway obstruction and collapse during sleep.

Clinical Collaboration

Working with researchers and clinicians specializing in sleep medicine and respiratory physiology.

Therapeutic Innovation

Developing strategies that aim to address structural and physiological contributors to airway dysfunction.
