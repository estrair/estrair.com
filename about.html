About EstrAir

EstrAir is developing targeted airway therapies for symptoms that emerge during menopause, including cough, dryness, voice change, and sleep disruption.

These conditions affect millions of women, yet the airway remains an overlooked area of women's health with no approved targeted treatments.

Our focus is localized, science-driven approaches that address symptoms at their source.

Investigational only. Not FDA approved.
